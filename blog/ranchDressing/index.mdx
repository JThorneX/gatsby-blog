### Listen, I get it. You hate ranch dressing.

But you actually don't. You hate the gelatinous, vinegary, poorly seasoned ranch dressing you get in stores and restaurants. You don't hate homemade ranch dressing and you _especially_ don't hate homemade Babi (pronounced "bubbie") ranch dressing.

| :--------------------------------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------------------- |
| 1 cup                                                            | Sour Cream &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; |

1. In a large jar, put all ingredients in and shake very hard. Use whisk if it didn't mix well.
